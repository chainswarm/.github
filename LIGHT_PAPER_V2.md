## The Opportunity

Blockchain intelligence is a multi-billion dollar market dominated by three centralised players: Chainalysis, Elliptic, and TRM Labs. They charge enterprises hundreds of thousands of dollars annually for access to risk scoring and compliance tools. Regular users? Locked out entirely.

But the market is shifting:

**Regulatory pressure is accelerating.** MiCA in Europe, Travel Rule in the US, KYC requirements spreading globally. Every exchange, wallet, and DeFi protocol will need compliance tooling — not someday, but now.

**AI agents are becoming real crypto participants.** Autonomous trading bots, DeFi protocols, and smart wallets are executing transactions without human oversight. These agents need to answer one critical question before every interaction: *Is this address safe?*

**Users want answers.** Who is this wallet? Where did these funds come from? Is this address connected to known scammers? People are curious about the on-chain world, but current tools are either too expensive or too technical.

Chain Insights captures this opportunity by building the **open, decentralised intelligence layer** — accessible to everyone from curious individuals to autonomous AI agents.

## What Can You Do With Chain Insights?

### Investigate a Hack in Real-Time

A protocol gets exploited. Within minutes, you're tracking the attacker's every move. Watch as they attempt to fragment funds across dozens of wallets. See when they bridge to other chains. Get alerted the moment stolen assets hit an exchange where they might be frozen.

You're not waiting for someone else's report. You're running the investigation yourself — or your agent is running it for you, 24/7, updating you as the situation unfolds.

### Due Diligence Before a Large OTC Deal

You're about to receive a significant transfer from a counterparty you don't know well. Before you accept, you need to know: Where did their money come from? Are they connected to flagged entities? Will accepting these funds contaminate your own wallet's reputation?

The agent maps the complete history. You see exactly what you're getting into before you commit.

### Monitor a DAO Treasury for Insider Threats

A community treasury holds millions in assets. You set up continuous monitoring — not just for outflows, but for subtle patterns that precede theft. Unusual authorization patterns. New addresses appearing in the approval chain. Connections forming between treasury signers and known bad actors.

The agent watches for the warning signs that humans miss.

### Verify Counterparties Before Every Swap

Your trading agent is about to execute a large swap. The counterparty looks legitimate, but is it? The agent checks in milliseconds: sanctioned address? Connected to recent exploits? History of rug pulls? Suspicious fund origins?

Every transaction your agent makes is informed. No blind trust. No nasty surprises.

### Track a Ransomware Gang's Cash-Out Operation

A criminal group has been collecting ransoms for months. Now they're trying to cash out. You're following every wallet they control, watching them try to fragment funds, use mixers, hop across bridges. The moment anything hits an exchange with KYC, you know.

Build the evidence trail. Identify the cash-out points. Support recovery efforts.

### Protect a Protocol From Sanctioned Fund Contamination

Your DeFi protocol can't afford to process transactions from sanctioned addresses — the regulatory risk is existential. Every deposit, every interaction gets screened in real-time. Flagged sources are blocked before they contaminate your liquidity pools.

Compliance without friction. Protection without manual review.

### Detect Insider Trading Patterns Before a Token Launch

Strange accumulation happening in wallets connected to team members. Unusual fund flows between "unrelated" addresses that share suspicious patterns. The agent surfaces these connections — letting communities identify potential insider activity before, not after, they get rugged.

### Map the Complete Network of a Suspicious Entity

You've identified one address of interest. But who else do they control? Who do they transact with? What's the full picture of their on-chain operations?

The agent expands from a single address to the complete network — related wallets, frequent counterparties, fund sources, ultimate destinations. One address becomes a full intelligence map.

### Real-Time Alerts When Your Exposure Changes

Your wallet interacted with an address six months ago. Today, that address got flagged as connected to a major hack. Your risk profile just changed — and you need to know.

The agent monitors retroactively, not just in real-time. When the risk landscape shifts, you're informed immediately.

### Autonomous Agent Protection Layer

Your AI trading agent operates 24/7 across multiple protocols. It needs to make trust decisions constantly — and it can't wait for human approval. Chain Insights becomes its intelligence layer: every counterparty checked, every transaction risk-assessed, every interaction informed by network-wide intelligence.

The agentic economy needs agents that can protect themselves. Chain Insights makes that possible.

## Built for the Agentic Economy

The future of crypto is autonomous. AI agents are already trading, providing liquidity, managing portfolios, and interacting with DeFi protocols — all without human intervention.

These agents face a critical challenge: they need to make trust decisions in milliseconds. Is this counterparty safe? Is this token legitimate? Has this address been involved in suspicious activity?

### Agent Communication Protocol (ACP)

Chain Insights is designed from the ground up for agent-to-agent communication. Through the Agent Communication Protocol, AI agents can:

- **Query risk intelligence** — Get instant risk scores for any address before transacting
- **Verify counterparties** — Check if a potential trading partner has suspicious history
- **Monitor positions** — Receive alerts when risk profiles change for addresses they're exposed to
- **Share intelligence** — Contribute observations back to the network, improving collective knowledge

ACP enables a new paradigm: agents that are not just autonomous, but *informed*. An agent that can check counterparty risk before every swap. A lending protocol that can assess borrower reputation in real-time. A wallet that warns users before they send funds to a known scammer.

### Butler: Your Personal Blockchain Intelligence Agent

Butler is the user-facing manifestation of Chain Insights intelligence. Think of it as a personal assistant that understands blockchain forensics.

Ask Butler anything:

- *"Who is this wallet?"*
- *"Where did these funds come from?"*
- *"Is it safe to interact with this address?"*
- *"Show me everyone this wallet has transacted with"*
- *"Track this stolen fund and tell me when it hits an exchange"*
- *"Monitor this scammer cluster and alert me to new activity"*

Butler translates complex blockchain analysis into plain language. It doesn't require you to understand graph theory or transaction parsing. Just ask your question and get an answer you can act on.

Butler isn't just reactive. It proactively monitors your interests, alerts you to relevant developments, and helps you navigate the on-chain world safely. It's the intelligence layer that sits between you and the complexity of blockchain data.

### x402 Agent Gateway

For AI agents operating autonomously, Chain Insights provides the x402 gateway — a pay-per-query API designed for machines.

No accounts. No subscriptions. No human approval required. Just instant, permissionless access to blockchain intelligence at the moment of decision.

A trading bot checks counterparty risk before executing a swap. A lending protocol assesses borrower history before approving a loan. A smart wallet warns its owner before sending funds to a flagged address. All autonomous. All in real-time.

The x402 gateway uses micropayments, so agents pay only for the intelligence they consume. This makes blockchain intelligence economically viable for high-frequency, low-margin operations that would be impossible with traditional subscription models.

## Token Economics & Revenue

Chain Insights generates value through multiple revenue streams, creating sustainable economics for token holders.

### Revenue Streams

**Query Revenue**
Every intelligence query generates fees. Risk scores, funds tracking, relationship mapping, threat detection — each request pays into the network. As adoption grows across users and agents, query volume compounds.

**Butler Subscriptions**
Premium Butler features — advanced monitoring, priority alerts, investigation reports — operate on subscription tiers. Users pay for deeper intelligence access and proactive agent capabilities.

**x402 Agent Micropayments**
AI agents pay per query through the x402 gateway. No subscriptions, pure usage-based revenue. As the agentic economy scales, this becomes the dominant revenue driver. Thousands of agents making millions of queries daily.

**Label Licensing**
The network continuously discovers and verifies address labels — exchange wallets, scammer addresses, protocol treasuries. This dataset is valuable to compliance providers, other intelligence platforms, and research institutions.

**Investigation Services**
Premium forensic reports for stolen funds recovery, legal proceedings, and due diligence. High-margin services for users who need court-admissible evidence and expert analysis.

### Value Flow & Token Buyback-and-Burn

```
Revenue ──┬──▶ Network Operations (infrastructure, validation, development)
          ├──▶ Token Buyback & Burn (permanent supply reduction)
          └──▶ Innovation Fund (R&D, chain expansion, partnerships)
```

**Our commitment: Buy back tokens from the open market and burn them permanently.**

This is core to our tokenomics — not a vague promise, but a deflationary mechanism built into how we operate.

**Phased Approach:**

In the early stages, revenue is reinvested into development. Building robust infrastructure, expanding chain support, and scaling the network requires capital. We're transparent about this — dev funds come first while we're growing.

Once the network reaches a sustainable FDV threshold, buyback-and-burn begins. A portion of all revenue goes directly to:
1. Purchasing tokens from the open market
2. Burning them permanently — removing them from circulation forever

The burn program continues until a significant percentage of total supply is removed. The exact timeline depends on market conditions and network growth, but our intention is clear: **get to burns as fast as responsibly possible.**

**The Flywheel:**
- More users and agents → more queries
- More queries → more revenue
- More revenue → more buybacks and burns
- Reduced supply → stronger token value
- Stronger token → more attention → more users and agents

Every query, every subscription, every investigation report contributes to permanent supply reduction. Token holders benefit directly from network growth through deflationary pressure.
